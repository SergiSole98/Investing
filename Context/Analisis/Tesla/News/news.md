- [2026-04-09] Trump Reciprocal Tariffs Take Effect: +34% on China, +20% on EU, +24% on Japan — Source: Reuters
  Trump's reciprocal tariff schedule officially took effect on April 9, layering 34% on Chinese imports, 20% on EU imports, and 24% on Japanese imports atop the existing 25% tariff on all imported cars and auto parts. China retaliated immediately with 125% tariffs on US goods. Tesla warned in SEC filings it is "exposed to retaliatory tariffs on exports." Practical impacts: Tesla stopped taking new orders for Model S and Model X in China. Giga Shanghai sources 90%+ of components locally, partially insulating the China Model 3/Y segment, but specialty components (rare earth magnets, semiconductor inputs) retain China sourcing with estimated per-unit cost headwinds in the high single to low double digits.
