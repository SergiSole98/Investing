These positions are mechanically driven by index inclusion and do not represent directional investment decisions. They provide structural price stability but amplify index-level selling pressure in broad market selloffs (tariff-driven equity decline would reduce passive demand proportionally).

Dealer gamma positioning at $350 creates a magnet effect; moves through $350 or $300 would trigger hedging flows that amplify directional momentum.
